# Kudo Advisory website, backlog (on hold)

Parked on 2026-06-02. Revisit in a few weeks. These are peer-inspired
differentiators identified from reviewing other AI advisory sites. The site
is already feature-complete vs peers; these make a boutique look established.

## Priority: build #1 and #4 first

1. **Named, visual proprietary framework** (highest value)
   Productise the assessment's six foundations of AI readiness (strategy,
   governance, operating model, data, talent, leadership) into a named model,
   e.g. "The Kudo AI Readiness Framework", with a clean diagram on a dedicated
   section or page. Makes the approach tangible, quotable, and good for SEO/AEO.

2. **"Industries we serve" strip**
   Icon strip: telco, utilities, healthcare, energy, financial services,
   property, public sector. At-a-glance sector credibility.

3. **Credibility / impact metrics band on the homepage**
   Use real facts only (no fabrication): 25+ years advising boards, 5 regions
   or continents, 6 advisory services, industries from telco to utilities.

4. **Responsible AI principles block** (quick, on-brand)
   Short principles: independence, governance-first, human accountability,
   transparency. Fits the UAE governance angle and regulated-industry buyers.

## Deliberately NOT doing
- Vendor/partner badges (AWS, Microsoft, etc.): undercuts the independent,
  vendor-neutral positioning.
- Implementation/MLOps/engineering services: advisory-only by design.
- SOC2/ISO badges: low relevance for pure advisory.
